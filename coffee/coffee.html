Koretto........................................$55

After making an espresso, a tablespoon of strong alcohol is added to it, most often whiskey or brandy, less often gin, grappa or vodka. Use in the cold season after meals, sometimes with cane sugar or honey.

Irish.............................................$52

Espresso with strong alcohol and whipped cream. In the classic version of the sugar is not put, but there are recipes with the addition of various toppings, for example, chocolate or cream.

Glasse.........................................$40

Espresso with a scoop of ice cream and small chocolate chips. They drink it in the hot season chilled, served in tall glasses with a straw.
