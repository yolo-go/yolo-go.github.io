About company Rolleiflex

Rollei is one of the oldest photographic equipment companies. Rollei is a reputable company whose name is valued among professional photographers. Rollei has created the Rolleiflex medium format camera, which has been and remains a cult. The company continues to produce cameras of this series, lenses and accessories for them.

Company founders Paul Franke and Reinhold Heideck wanted to create high-quality cameras that are easy to use. They wanted to make cameras not only for professional photographers, but also for a wide range of lovers. As a result, in 1929 the legendary, iconic two-lens Rolleiflex camera was born, which became the standard for professional photographers and a dream for advanced amateurs.

The dual lens camera marked the beginning of a long product line. The next step in the development of the company was the production of 35-mm Rollei 35 cameras in 1966. and, further, the Rollei SL66 medium format SLR camera. Subsequently, the company created the first electronically controlled medium format Rolleiflex SLX camera - a 35 mm SLR camera with a replaceable SL2000F magazine and projectors that have remained unique to date.
